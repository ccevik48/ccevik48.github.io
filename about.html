About Me

I am Can, a software developer with a degree in Computer Engineering. Math was my favorite subject throughout my educational career, which led me to pursue a degree in engineering. My background in programming started with learning the basics of C and C++ in introductory courses, then learning about Java and principles of object oriented programming. Through my volunteer work, personal projects, and course work, I gained experience to be able to land my first internship at Skyline Communications, where I became familiar with the agile methodology, version control software (i.e. TortoiseSVN), and working in a team setting; I also gained experience in C# and XML. I landed a second internship at Social Mobile soon after graduating, where I also gained experience in C# and Angular. I am now currently a software developer at General Motors, and hope to contribute in an impactful way through working as one team by writing high quality code.
